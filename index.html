About my way to work

Back when I was still getting my bearings in 2024, a simple academic task turned into a mini-marathon: my classmates spent an hour grappling with a prime-checking function while I hit play on Brain.fm, tackled the logic in five minutes flat, and celebrated with a brain-food snack. I believe in setting bite-sized goals, keeping lines of communication wide open, and embracing each new puzzle with curiosity and a subtle grin—after all, the best solutions often spark when you least expect them.

My Vision as Software Engineer

Have you ever watched your own brain-food truck shiver in the cold—and thought, “Time to stir things up”? That was me at Nockherberg in 2022, when freezing temps emptied the line. Instead of packing up, I flipped the script: launched a pre-booking model, boosted margins, and kept bowls—and smiles—coming.

Since then I’ve poured that “never-stop” drive into every venture: founding Way2Nuts (Oct 2020–Mar 2025), scaling a lean team of four, and helping PepeShows climb into Google’s Top-3 while growing 50K+ followers. Offstage, I’ve spent two decades in the circus—Cyr-wheel is my main act—so I know how to spot each person’s superpower, juggle resources with flair, and cushion my team against stress so creativity can roll freely. (Feel free to catch a few of my circus highlights on Instagram: @chris.)
